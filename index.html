The Story

From midwife to
maximalist creator

In March 2020 I moved into our 1960s Cambridgeshire cottage, took one look at the magnolia living room, and reached for the pink paint. I had no idea it would change everything.

Farrow & Ball reshared my photo. Then it cascaded. By April 2021 I'd reached 310K followers and made the leap from NHS midwife to full-time creator. Now I'm here with 715K of you, and we're all in it together — the bolder, the better.

My home is my canvas and my business. It's also the place where my husband Chris says no (a lot), and where Clemmie and Minnie have the most joyful, colourful childhood I can give them. More is more, and we're not sorry.
